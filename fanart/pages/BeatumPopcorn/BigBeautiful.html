RootPain's Commentary

Beatum? Who is this handsome man?
Man, this is still one of absolute favorites.
As usual I love the colors, and this piece specifically
awakened my love for the mixture of yellow and pink
the yellow of his sunflower and his jacket combined
with the soft pinks really gives this piece such
a comfy and magical feeling

In general, I feel like a lot of the more simple pieces Beatum ended up doing
for me always ended up being some of my favorite fanarts I'd ever receive
I really dig simple stuff, but there's always a lot of detail to complement
the simplicity aswell

This is also the first time Beatum ever redesigned my iconic jacket
He took the hoodie out and gave it a more futuristic approach
And I really like it
Every time I see the way he draws Big Whiskey it makes me wanna go back
and try to redesign Big Whiskey again, but that's a story for another time.

Either way, that was it for now
I'll be back with more, and be back for more, so keep makin' and sendin' em
so I can look at em' and hang em' on my fridge
I'll see yer' asses soon, see ya!
