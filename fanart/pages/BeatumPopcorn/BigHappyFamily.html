RootPain's Commentary

Look who it is again, it is I, RootPain!
And once again, bringing you more fanart!
and once again, from BeatumPopcorn!
Or BeatumPop as he's known now.
There's a LOT going on here, and these are some of
my favorites fanarts too, so let's talk about it!

I think it's good for me to give some context first.
Back when I first started talking to Beatum, I started this joke of me shipping
his and my character together, and it was a fairly simple, innocent joke
but then, for some reason, it didn't feel wrong, in fact, we liked it
so slowly but surely, something that started as a joke, quickly turned into
something I'm legitimately fond of, and this led to the shipping of
Big Whiskey and Caira, and later, the creation of their little future
family as you see above

These pieces are Beatum's interpretation of them in the future
and what their children would look like

I really like old Big Whiskey, he looks like a cool dad that went through
everything and is now just chilling and enjoy the good things in life
and I specially like how you can see a few more scars around his face
too, and his iconic devil horn's big ass hair

And Caira? Oh boy! Just look at her!
She definitely gives a "young mom" vibe, like, she's still fun and goofy
but if push comes to shove, she won't hesitate on putting her foot down
to teach some discipline, and I also dig how her outfit feels like a more
casual take on her usual attire

There's also their children, the little girl (which has no name yet)
and Little Whiskey (which also has no real name yet)

I came up with Little Whiskey way earlier than I did with the girl
and his appearance and personality heavily resemble a younger
Big Whiskey, as he is shown to have a bad temper, and his jacket is just
Big Whiskey's original jacket, with the in-universe explanation being
that Big Whiskey gave his old jacket to his son, due to the fact
it didn't fit him anymore.

The little girl is more passive by comparision, she's more upbeat
when in comparision to Little Whiskey, and although she's scared
of getting in trouble, she's always finding a way to get herself
in mischief, she just can't help it

I really like these pieces, and the little stories we made with these
characters makes me really happy
They seem happy together, and I'm happy about it :)

But that's it for now I promise I'm not slacking, I'm just feeling down
I'll come back soon, don't lose hope
I hope to see you soon, see ya!

A Dreamer

RootPain
